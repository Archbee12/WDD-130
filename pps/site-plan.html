About Portfolio

The website is personal, and will be consisting heavily in the programmer aesthetics with the major purpose and aim to attract potential clients and showcase my on-going projects as a student in BYU-I. Moreover, my extensive knowledge of recognized technical standards is complemented by my proficiency in modern building practices, ensuring that every aspect of your website is finely tuned to perfection.

The zeal of fullfiling a professional experience in programming and creating a responsive websites will put potential employers and clients alike to get everything they need to know in a glance without having to scan the pages. This is my goals.
